Audio issues on iPhone 16 Pro affect calls, music, and videos. Fix speaker problems with these solutions.

## Common iPhone 16 Pro Speaker Issues

- No sound from speakers
- Crackling or distortion
- Muffled audio
- One speaker not working
- Sound cutting in and out

## 9 Fixes for iPhone 16 Pro Speaker

### 1. Check Audio Settings
Settings > Sound. Ensure Ring/Silent switch not on silent. Check volume slider.

### 2. Clean Speaker Grilles
Use soft brush to remove lint and debris from speaker holes at bottom.

### 3. Remove Case and Screen Protector
Audio can be muffled by thick cases or protectors covering speakers.

### 4. Test with Different Apps
Play music in Apple Music, then YouTube. If one app works, it's app-specific issue.

### 5. Restart iPhone
Quick restart clears audio service glitches.

### 6. Check Bluetooth
Settings > Bluetooth. Disconnect any paired devices that might be routing audio elsewhere.

### 7. Reset All Settings
Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Reset > Reset All Settings.

### 8. Update iOS
Audio driver updates in iOS versions. Update via Settings > General > Software Update.

### 9. Contact Apple Support
Hardware issue if no fixes work. Check warranty status at support.apple.com.

## Testing iPhone Speakers

Dial *#0*# for hardware test (some carriers). Or use Apple Support app for speaker tests.

## FAQ

**Q: Why does my iPhone 16 Pro speaker crackle?**
A: Debris in speaker grille, software issue, or hardware damage. Clean first, then restart.

**Q: How do I know if it's the speaker or software?**
A: Test with safemode restart. If speakers work in safemode, third-party app is conflict.

## Conclusion
Most speaker issues resolve with cleaning or restart. Hardware problems need Apple support.
