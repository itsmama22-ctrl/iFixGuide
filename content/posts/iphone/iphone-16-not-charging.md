iPhone 16 charging issues prevent your phone from staying powered. Fix charging problems with these solutions.

## Common iPhone 16 Charging Problems

- iPhone won't charge at all
- Charging slowly
- Stops charging at 80%
- "Accessory not supported" error
- Wireless charging not working
- MagSafe charger not connecting

## 10 Fixes for iPhone 16 Charging Issues

### 1. Check Cable and Adapter
Inspect Lightning cable fraying. Test with different Apple charger.

### 2. Clean Charging Port
Use SIM ejector tool or soft brush to remove lint from port.

### 3. Force Restart
Hold Volume Up, Down, Side button until Apple logo.

### 4. Check Power Source
Try different wall outlet. Computer USB may not provide enough power.

### 5. Remove Case
MagSafe and wireless charging affected by thick cases.

### 6. Update iOS
Charging driver updates in iOS. Update via Settings > General > Software Update.

### 7. Reset All Settings
Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Reset > Reset All Settings.

### 8. Disable Optimized Charging
Settings > Battery > Battery Health > Optimized Charging > Off. Test if this is blocking charge.

### 9. DFU Restore
Complete restore for persistent charging issues.

### 10. Check Liquid Detection
If "Liquid Detected" appears, dry port naturally. Don't use cable until dry.

## iPhone 16 Fast Charging Requirements

- 20W+ Apple adapter for fast charging
- USB-C to Lightning cable
- MagSafe charger: 15W max
- Always use Apple-certified accessories

## FAQ

**Q: Why does iPhone 16 stop charging at 80%?**
A: Optimized Charging learns your routine. Disable in Battery Settings if unwanted.

**Q: Does wireless charging affect battery health?**
A: Slightly. But Apple's MagSafe is optimized. Use official accessories only.

## Conclusion
Most charging issues resolve with cable replacement or port cleaning. Hardware problems need Apple support.
