Camera issues on iPhone 18 Pro prevent capturing moments. Fix problems with these 11 solutions.

## Common iPhone 18 Pro Camera Issues

- Black camera screen
- Camera app crashing
- Photos out of focus
- Lens not responding
- Video stabilization issues
- Night mode failing

## 11 Fixes for iPhone 18 Pro Camera

### 1. Force Close Camera App
Swipe up from bottom, close Camera app, reopen.

### 2. Restart iPhone
Standard restart clears camera glitches.

### 3. Check Storage Space
Need minimum 2GB free. Settings > General > iPhone Storage.

### 4. Update iOS
Camera improvements in updates. Settings > General > Software Update.

### 5. Clean Camera Lens
Microfiber cloth gently. Smudges affect quality.

### 6. Test Different Modes
Try all camera modes. One failing indicates specific lens.

### 7. Check Camera Permissions
Settings > Privacy > Camera > Enable Camera access.

### 8. Reset Camera Settings
Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Reset > Reset All Settings.

### 9. Reset Network Settings
Network issues affect camera processing. Settings > Reset > Reset Network Settings.

### 10. Disable Computational Photography
Settings > Camera > Photographic Styles > Standard (test if causing issues).

### 11. DFU Restore
Complete restore for persistent software camera issues.

## FAQ

**Q: Why is my iPhone 18 Pro camera blurry?**
A: Dirty lens, optical stabilization issue, or software bug. Clean lens and restart first.

**Q: Does iOS 19 improve camera quality?**
A: Updates include tuning improvements. Keep updated.

## Conclusion
Most camera issues resolve with restart or storage cleanup. Persistent issues need Apple service.
