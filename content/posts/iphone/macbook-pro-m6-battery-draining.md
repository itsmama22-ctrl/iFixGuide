MacBook Pro M6 battery drain frustrates users. Extend battery life with these 12 solutions.

## Why MacBook Pro M6 Battery Drains Quickly

- M6 Pro chip high performance
- ProMotion 120Hz mini-LED display
- 5G modem in some models
- macOS Sequoia early bugs
- High brightness always on
- Multiple external displays

## 12 Fixes for MacBook Pro M6 Battery Drain

### 1. Enable Low Power Mode
System Settings > Battery > Low Power Mode.

### 2. Reduce Display Brightness
Function keys > Lower brightness or use Control Center.

### 3. Turn Off 5G/LTE
System Settings > Network > Manage Mobile Data > Off.

### 4. Use Energy Saver
System Settings > Battery > Energy Saver mode.

### 5. Limit Login Items
System Settings > General > Login Items > Remove unused apps.

### 6. Check Activity Monitor
Applications > Utilities > Activity Monitor > Energy tab > Close high drain apps.

### 7. Update macOS
Battery optimizations in updates. System Settings > Software Update.

### 8. Disable Background Apps
System Settings > General > Background App Refresh > Off.

### 9. Reduce Connected Displays
Multiple displays drain power. Use single display when possible.

### 10. Reset SMC
Intel: Option + Control + Shift + Power 10 seconds.
Apple Silicon: Shut down > Wait 30 seconds > Power on.

### 11. Check Battery Health
System Settings > Battery > Battery Health > Service Battery warning.

### 12. Apple Service
Battery health below 80% needs replacement.

## Optimal MacBook Pro M6 Settings

- Low Power Mode: ON
- Display brightness: 50-70%
- Connected displays: 1 when mobile
- Background apps: OFF
- Auto-Lock: 2 minutes

## FAQ

**Q: How long should MacBook Pro M6 battery last?**
A: 15-20 hours with normal use. Heavy tasks drain faster.

**Q: Does macOS Sequoia drain battery?**
A: Early versions may have bugs. Update to latest version.

## Conclusion
Most battery issues resolve with Low Power Mode and settings. Hardware issues need service.
