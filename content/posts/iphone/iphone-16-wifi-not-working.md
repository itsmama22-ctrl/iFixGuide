WiFi connectivity problems on iPhone 16 disrupt your internet. Fix WiFi issues with these proven solutions.

## Common iPhone 16 WiFi Problems

- WiFi won't turn on
- Can't find network
- Connected but no internet
- WiFi keeps disconnecting
- Slow WiFi speeds
- "Authentication failed" error

## 10 Fixes for iPhone 16 WiFi Issues

### 1. Restart Router and iPhone
Power cycle your router. Restart iPhone.

### 2. Toggle WiFi
Settings > WiFi > Off, wait 10 seconds, On.

### 3. Forget Network
Settings > WiFi > tap network > Forget This Network. Reconnect fresh.

### 4. Reset Network Settings
Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Reset > Reset Network Settings.

### 5. Check Router Settings
Ensure router firmware updated. Check 2.4GHz/5GHz band compatibility.

### 6. Change DNS
Settings > WiFi > network > Configure DNS > Manual. Try 8.8.8.8 (Google) or 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare).

### 7. Update iOS
WiFi driver updates in iOS. Keep updated.

### 8. Reset Location & Privacy
Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Reset > Reset Location & Privacy.

### 9. Check for Interference
Other electronics can interfere. Move away from microwave, cordless phone.

### 10. Contact ISP
If issue is only with one network, ISP may be blocking or throttling.

## Improving WiFi Speed

- Move closer to router
- Forget and reconnect to force better band selection
- Disable WiFi Assist (Settings > Cellular > WiFi Assist)
- Reset router if speeds suddenly dropped

## FAQ

**Q: Why does iPhone 16 WiFi keep disconnecting?**
A: Usually router issue or network overload. Try resetting network settings or different network.

**Q: Does iOS 18 improve WiFi?**
A: Updates can improve WiFi stability. Always update to latest iOS version.

## Conclusion
Most WiFi issues resolve with forget/reconnect or network settings reset. Router issues need ISP support.
